PRATYAKSH

Pratyaksh the street theater society of NIEC, has always been committed and lived up to the objectives. What a Street Play group is meant for? Creating social awareness and taking art to the next level. The aim of this society is to spread various issues amongst the masses and to make realize the realities and harsh truths of world around.

They have been fortunate enough to represent NIEC at an annual street theatre festival 'V4V' organized by The United Nations (UN) Information Centre, Delhi Technical University.

They also made their presence felt at various College fests in Delhi & NCR region and brought laurel to the Institute.
