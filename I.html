Northern India Engineering College

Sashatri Park, New Delhi





LABS

Labs are well equipped with instruments ,but due to grace of education system , there is no need of yours to learn about them practically .rote learning is sufficient and best way to pass the exams.

LIBRARY

The library is updated and well furnished with lots of books including new editions of some books .The three floor building serves as restroom for tired one and knowledge hub for geeky freaks. Library is equipped with wi-fi but accessible only by boys hostellers and faculties. Hostellers might get advantage over there day-skii colleagues.
